3. System: Car Insurance
Models the business logic between a client, their vehicle, and the insurance provider's policy management.

Classes & Structure
InsuranceCompany: The governing entity. Attributes: name. Methods: createPolicy(), processClaim().

Customer: The policyholder. Attributes: customerID, address. Method: applyForPolicy().

Policy: The contract. Attributes: policyNumber, premium. Method: calculateRisk().

Vehicle: The insured object. Attributes: model. Method: getVehicleValue().

Claim: Represents an accident report. Attributes: claimID, status. Method: approveClaim().

Key Relationships
Association: Customer owns a Vehicle.

Dependency: InsuranceCompany depends on Policy data to processClaim().

Association Class: A Policy can be seen as the link between a Customer and the InsuranceCompany.
