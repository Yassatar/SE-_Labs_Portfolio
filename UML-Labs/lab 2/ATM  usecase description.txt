1. System: ATM (Automated Teller Machine)This system models the interaction between a bank customer and the hardware interface for financial operations.

Actors
Customer: The primary actor who initiates transactions.

Bank: The secondary actor (system) that processes and authorizes requests.

Use Case DescriptionsAuthentification: The mandatory entry point to verify the customer's identity.

Withdraw: The core process of taking cash from the account.

Relation: Includes Authentification (must login to withdraw).

Invalid PIN: A specialized scenario triggered during failed login attempts.

Relation: Includes Authentification.

Sold (Balance): Allows the user to view their remaining balance.

Reciep (Receipt): An optional printout provided after a withdrawal.

Relation: Extends Withdraw (only occurs if the user chooses it).
