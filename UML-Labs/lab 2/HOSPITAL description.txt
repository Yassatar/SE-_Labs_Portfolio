 2. System: Hospital ManagementFocuses on the coordination between patients and medical staff for healthcare services.

Actors
Patient: The user seeking medical appointments and records.

Doctor: The medical professional managing prescriptions and consultations.

Use Case DescriptionsAuthentification: Secure login for both patients and medical staff.

Book Appointment: The process of scheduling a visit with a doctor.

Relation: Includes Authentification.

Cancel Appointment: Allows a user to remove a scheduled visit.

View Medical Record: Secure access to patient history, used by both actors.

Relation: Extends Book Appointment (often reviewed during the booking context).

Prescribe Medication: Action exclusive to the Doctor to provide treatments.
