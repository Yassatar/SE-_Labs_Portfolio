3. System: Car InsuranceA workflow-oriented system managing policy purchases and accident claims.

Actors
Customer: The client who buys insurance and reports incidents.
Insurance Agent: Manages the administrative side of policies.
Claim Adjuster: Evaluates the validity of insurance claims.
Use Case DescriptionsLogin: The central security gateway for all users.
Get Quote: Initiated by the customer to see pricing.
Relation: Includes Login.
Buy Policy: The formal purchase of insurance coverage.
Relation: Includes Login.
Claim: Reporting an accident to the company.
Relation: Includes Login.Photos: Uploading evidence for a claim.
Relation: Extends Claim (optional supporting evidence).
Manage Policies: Exclusive to the Insurance Agent to oversee client contracts.
Process Claim: Action taken by the Claim Adjuster to approve or deny a request.
