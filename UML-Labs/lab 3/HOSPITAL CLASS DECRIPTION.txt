2. System: Medical Clinic (Hospital)
Focuses on the management of clinical staff, patient care, and medical record persistence.

Classes & Structure
Staff (Abstract): Base class for employees. Attributes: staffID, name. Method: login().

Doctor: Inherits from Staff. Attributes: specialization. Method: prescribeMedication().

Patient: The service recipient. Attributes: patientID, contactInfo. Method: viewMedicalRecord().

Appointment: Manages scheduling. Attributes: date, time. Method: cancelAppointment().

MedicalRecord: Stores history. Attributes: recordID, history (List). Method: updateHistory().

Key Relationships
Inheritance: Doctor is a specialization of Staff.

Aggregation: A Doctor is associated with multiple Appointments.

Composition: A Patient has a strictly bound MedicalRecord.
