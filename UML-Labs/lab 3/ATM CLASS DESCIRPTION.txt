1. System: ATM (Automated Teller Machine)
This system models secure financial transactions and the relationship between bank accounts and physical hardware.

Classes & Structure
ATM: The central interface. Contains attributes like location and atmID. It manages methods such as identifyCard() and dispenseCash().

Customer: Represents the user. Attributes: name, customerID. Methods: insertCard(), enterPIN().

Card: Validates access. Attributes: cardNumber. Method: validatePIN().

Account: An abstract representation of funds. Attributes: accountNumber, balance.

Saving Account: Inherits from Account. Adds interestRate and the method applyInterest().

Transaction: Records operations. Attributes: transactionID, date. Method: execute().

Key Relationships
Inheritance: Saving Account inherits from Account.

Association: Customer uses the ATM and owns a Card.

Composition: An Account is composed of multiple Transactions.
